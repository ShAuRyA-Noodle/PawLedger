## Discovery questions (qualify the deal)

- "What's your current CSR commitment for FY26?"
- "How many causes are in your portfolio?" (5+ = good fit; <3 = harder sell)
- "Who internally signs off on CSR-2 paperwork?" (find the user, not the buyer)
- "What's the biggest pain in your current CSR reporting?" (reporting cadence, photo evidence, audit prep — listen)
- "Have you used Sattva, Give.do, or a similar platform?" (price-anchor against incumbents)
- "What's the timeline to spend the FY26 budget?" (urgency)
