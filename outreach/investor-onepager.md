## Problem

- 15–70M street animals in India. Largest population on earth. No transparent platform compounds donor capital toward their care.
- Existing platforms (Ketto, Milaap, ImpactGuru) optimise for one-time medical campaigns, not recurring sponsorship.
- Animal charities globally have a trust crisis: ASPCA spending exposé (CBS), HSUS $15.75M RICO, 1,022 fake Instagram rescues generating 572M views in 6 weeks (2024).
- Indian corporate CSR pool is ₹26,000+ Cr/yr; animal welfare is Schedule VII eligible but underserved.

## Wedge

Three defensible moats stacked into one product:

1. **Identifiable-victim recurring sponsorship** — what works at Best Friends ($25/mo Guardian Angels) and Charity:Water ("The Spring"), brought to India with INR/UPI rails and ₹300/mo sweet spot.
2. **Public on-ledger transparency** — every donation tagged at the line item with vendor + amount + photo proof, append-only, SHA-256 chained.
3. **Geo-located SOS rescue dispatch** — Uber-for-injured-strays. Citizen reports → nearest verified shelter → micro-funded vet care → outcome posted publicly.

## Market

- **TAM** (global animal welfare giving): ~$13–18B/yr; US ~$5–7B
- **SAM** (India urban pet-owning + animal-empathetic households): ~₹2,400–5,000 Cr/yr (~$300–600M)
- **SOM** (year 3): ₹120 Cr/yr ARR with 250k recurring donors + 25 enterprise CSR + 1k partner embeds

## Business model

- 4% platform fee on donations (vs Ketto 3–4.5%, Milaap 5%, GlobalGiving 8–15%)
- Payment processor fees pass through at cost
- Donor-cover-fee opt-in (never pre-checked; ~50% adoption)
- **CSR SaaS** — annual ₹2L base + 3% of routed CSR funds (the unlock)
- Embed widget — free for nonprofits, ₹999/mo for commercial partners
- Vet-credit marketplace (v1.5) — 10–15% take on vet bookings funded by donor balances

## Path to $200k MRR

- 50,000 recurring donors @ avg ₹350/mo (5% take) = ₹8.75 lakh/mo
- 5 enterprise CSR contracts @ ₹15 lakh/yr (3% of ₹3 Cr each) = ₹13.75 lakh/mo
- 200 partner-clinic embeds @ avg ₹3,000/mo flat + 5% volume = ₹6 lakh/mo
- 1,000 premium-tier donors @ ₹999 = ₹10 lakh/mo
- **Total ~₹38 lakh/mo (~$45k MRR) at 50k donors.** $200k MRR realistically 18–36 months with focused execution + capital.

## Defensibility

- **Trust moat compounds** — every shelter onboarded + every clean ledger entry raises switching cost for donors and creates a brand asset competitors can't shortcut.
