# Shelter cold-outreach templates

## Email 1 — first contact (use shelter founder's name)

Subject: A monthly recurring funding model for [Shelter Name] — quick intro

Hi [Name],

I run PawLedger — a recurring sponsorship platform for animal shelters in India. We're inviting 5 verified shelters into our pilot launching this month.

Quick pitch:
- Monthly sponsors at ₹100, ₹300, or ₹500 per donor — animal-by-animal, not generic
- Auto 80G receipts for every donor (no paperwork from you)
- T+1 payouts via Razorpay (T+0 for emergency cases via Cashfree)
- Public, hash-chained ledger for every rupee — solves the donor trust problem

Platform fee: 4% (compared to 5% on Milaap, 3–4.5% on Ketto). Payment processor fees pass through at cost.

I saw [specific thing about their work — last rescue you noticed, their Instagram, etc.]. Would love to feature [Animal Name] as one of our launch animals.

Open to a 15-minute call this week or next?

— [Your name]
PawLedger · Pvt Ltd · [your phone]

## Onboarding call agenda (30 min)

1. **The shelter's current funding mix** (5 min): one-time vs recurring, donor count, monthly raise
2. **Demo PawLedger dashboard** (10 min): animal CMS, sponsor signup flow, payout queue, public ledger
3. **Compliance check** (5 min): 12A, 80G, AWBI, bank account
4. **Pilot terms** (5 min): 0% platform fee for first 90 days for pilot shelters; standard 4% after
5. **Next steps** (5 min): IDfy KYC link, animal profile uploads, first sponsor go-live target

## What to bring to the call (for them)
- Section 8 / Trust / Society registration certificate
- 12A + 80G certificates (with expiry dates)
- AWBI recognition (if held)
- PAN + GSTIN
- Bank cancelled cheque
- 5–10 photos of the shelter + animals currently in care
- WhatsApp number for primary admin
