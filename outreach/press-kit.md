# PawLedger Press Kit

## Boilerplate (one paragraph)

**PawLedger** is a transparent, recurring sponsorship platform for animal welfare. Donors sponsor a specific rescued animal monthly (₹100–₹2,500), receive weekly photo updates from the shelter, and watch every rupee land on a public, tamper-evident ledger. PawLedger works only with verified shelters that hold valid 80G certificates and AWBI recognition. Built in India. Launched 2026. Run as a for-profit Pvt Ltd company; donations route directly to partner Section 8 NGOs who issue 80G receipts. Platform fee: 4%.
