# PawLedger — Brand Identity

## Name
**PawLedger** — paw (animal) + ledger (transparency, accountability). Trademark check pending; alt candidates if blocked: Karuna (Sanskrit "compassion"), Pawra (coined), Saathi ("companion"), PawProof.

## Tagline
**Compassion you can prove.**

Alternates:
- Every rupee, on the record.
- Sponsor a life. See every rupee.
- The transparent way to help animals.

## Voice & tone

- **Plain.** No charity-speak. "Sponsor Bruno for ₹300/month" not "Help us help these voiceless souls."
- **Calm.** Numbers + animal names. Never melodramatic.
- **Exact.** "Your ₹300 in March bought 14 lbs of food, 1 vet visit, 1 toy" beats "Your support means the world."
- **Anti-guilt.** Never "if you don't help, they die." We respect the donor's intelligence.
- **Anti-gore.** No graphic distress imagery. ASCI rules + just plain ethics.

## Color palette (OKLCH)

- **Ink** `oklch(20% 0.04 240)` — near-black with cool tint, primary text
- **Ledger Cream** `oklch(97% 0.02 80)` — warm off-white, page background
- **Marigold** `oklch(78% 0.16 70)` — primary action, donate CTA, brand accent (calls back to Indian marigold)
- **Sage** `oklch(72% 0.10 145)` — success, verified, "live ledger" indicators
- **Slate** `oklch(45% 0.02 240)` — secondary text, dividers
- **Coral** `oklch(70% 0.18 25)` — urgency only (SOS reports), use sparingly
- **Charcoal** `oklch(22% 0.01 0)` — dark mode primary surface

## Type

- **Display:** Instrument Serif (free, via Google Fonts) — emotive, editorial, used for animal names + hero
- **Body:** Inter Variable (free) — neutral, legible, rock-solid
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono (free) — ledger numbers, hashes, IDs

## Logo concept

Simple wordmark-only at v1. Lowercase **pawledger** in Instrument Serif, the "g" tail extending into a subtle paw mark. Marigold dot above the "i" optional later.

Icon-only mark for favicon: a stylized "P" whose counter forms a paw pad.

(Detailed logo system to be generated via the `taste-brandkit` skill in a follow-up.)

## Imagery direction

- **Yes:** real animals in natural light, eye-contact close-ups, quiet domestic moments (eating, sleeping, being petted), shelter caregivers' hands.
- **No:** stock photography, before/after gore, sad music, models with rescue dogs, AI-generated animal images (donors will sniff out and brand will collapse).

Every animal photo on the platform is captioned with date + name + photographer attribution. Provenance matters.

## Forbidden

- Pre-checked fee covers
- Auto-renewed merch upsells
- Gamified leaderboards by donor amount
- "Your sponsorship is at risk" loss-aversion copy
- Stock photos of unrelated animals
- Donor data sold or shared to third parties (zero exceptions)
- Pop-up exit-intent guilt modals
